## Moon Sighting in Brief

Islamic months traditionally begin with the sighting of the new moon.  
However, practices differ based on:

- Local vs global sighting
- Weather and visibility
- Use of astronomical calculations
- National religious authority decisions

These differences are documented here for educational clarity.
